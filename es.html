Abordo delArcandina

Alista tus maletas que ya vamos a partir, es un viaje inolvidable, nos vamos a divertir. A bordo del Arcandina vamos a navegar por ríos, lagos y mares del mundo enfrentando a los villanos como Avariciosus Máximus y Ratasura que contaminan el agua con plásticos, aceites, químicos y textiles. ¡Únete a esta gran aventura para cuidar el agua y proteger nuestro planeta!

Historias paraCuidar el Agua

I'm Cori, the sea lion from the Galápagos. I'm always watching over the sea and its mysteries. And I love water, because water is life. I want to share something exciting with you. Did you know you can learn so many curious things without leaving your home? For example, do you know what plastic islands are? You'll be amazed to learn how they form and how we can prevent them from growing. You can also discover simple yet powerful ways to help rivers no matter where you are.

There are many ways to learn how to care for water. If you enjoy watching videos, aboard the Arcandina, you'll find some with stories of heroes and villains. If you like listening to podcasts, you can find some with tips on how to care for water. And if you love reading, you'll also find a fun magazine packed with interesting information so we can learn together how to protect rivers and water sources.
