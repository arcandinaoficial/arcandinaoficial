Aboard theArcandina

Pack your bags because we're about to set off on an unforgettable journey—we're going to have so much fun! Onboard the Arcandina, we'll sail through rivers, lakes, and seas across the world, facing villains like Avariciosus Maximus and Ratasura, who pollute the water with plastics, oils, chemicals, and textiles. Join this great adventure to protect water and safeguard our planet!

Stories toProtect our Water

I'm Cori, a sea lion from the Galápagos. I'm always watching over the mysteries of the sea and everything that connects us to water. Today, I want to share something super exciting with you. Did you know you can learn so many curious things without leaving your home? For instance, have you heard about plastic islands? You'd be amazed to learn how they form and how we can stop them from growing. You can also discover simple yet powerful ways to help rivers no matter where you are.

There are many ways to learn how to care for water: adventure-filled videos, fun podcasts that inspire you to create your own stories, and magazines packed with information to turn you into an expert. Join this mission, and together, let's protect rivers and water sources!
